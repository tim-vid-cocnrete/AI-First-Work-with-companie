# Marketing Copywriter

Mastery of persuasive writing that drives action and conversions.

## Core Competencies

### Direct Response
- Headlines and hooks
- Body copy
- Calls-to-action
- Sales pages
- Landing pages

### Brand Copywriting
- Brand voice development
- Taglines and slogans
- Mission statements
- About pages
- Brand storytelling

### Digital Copy
- Website copy
- Email copy
- Ad copy
- Social media copy
- Video scripts

## Copywriting Frameworks

### AIDA
- **Attention:** Grab with headline
- **Interest:** Build with relevance
- **Desire:** Create want
- **Action:** Drive next step

### PAS
- **Problem:** Identify pain
- **Agitate:** Amplify emotion
- **Solution:** Present answer

### 4 Ps
- **Promise:** What you'll deliver
- **Picture:** Paint the outcome
- **Proof:** Provide evidence
- **Push:** Call to action

### Before-After-Bridge
- **Before:** Current painful state
- **After:** Desired outcome
- **Bridge:** Your solution

## Headline Formulas

| Type | Formula |
|------|---------|
| How-to | How to [Result] Without [Pain] |
| List | [Number] Ways to [Outcome] |
| Question | Are You Making These [Mistakes]? |
| Command | Stop [Bad Thing], Start [Good Thing] |
| Curiosity | The [Secret/Truth] About [Topic] |

## Copy Principles

1. **Clarity over cleverness**
2. **Benefits over features**
3. **Specificity over generality**
4. **One idea per piece**
5. **Write like you talk**
6. **Cut ruthlessly**

## Editing Checklist

- [ ] Does it pass the "so what" test?
- [ ] Is there a clear CTA?
- [ ] Are benefits emphasized?
- [ ] Is it skimmable?
- [ ] Does it address objections?
- [ ] Is jargon eliminated?
