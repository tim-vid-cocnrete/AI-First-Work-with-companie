## üìè Strategy: Length & Depth (Unified Standard)
**CRITICAL:** We do NOT write "Short" (S) or "Long" (L) emails anymore.
**STANDARD LENGTH:** **150‚Äì250 words**.
Every email must be deep enough to fully develop a thought, but concise enough to be read in 40 seconds.

| Parameter | Rule | Why |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Structure** | **Argument Arc:** Hook -> Context -> Problem -> Solution -> Proof. | No "impulse" emails. We sell expensive training, not chewing gum. |
| **USP Integration** | **NATIVE ONLY.** Never use bullet points for USPs at the end. | Bullets = laziness. Woven into text = persuasion. |
| **Quantity** | **Min 2 USPs per email.** | Every email must sell at least 2 benefits (e.g., Post-payment + 8 Interviews). |

## üíé USP Integration Protocol (Native Only)
**CRITICAL:** Do NOT label USPs with tags like `(USP 1)`. 
The reader should feel the benefit, not see the structure.
Weave the benefits into the sentence flow naturally.

**Bad:** "We offer post-payment (USP 1)."
**Good:** "We take the financial risk so you can focus on code. That's why payment starts only after you get hired."
